Our Mission

Helping Paws is a project dedicated to improving the quality of life for small- to medium-sized dogs that experience mobility challenges, including limited hind leg function or limb absence. The goal of the project is to design and distribute affordable assistive devices—such as adjustable dog wheelchairs to canine patients.

By leveraging 3D printing and cost-effective but durable materials, Helping Paws aims to reduce the financial barriers associated with existing mobility solutions on the market. The resulting designs prioritize adjustability and adaptability, allowing a single device platform to support multiple dog sizes and breeds but keeping functionality.

What We Provide

  • Custom wheelchairs and mobility carts
  • Prosthetic limbs and orthotics
  • Support harnesses and slings
  • Ongoing support and adjustments

Success Stories

Meet some of the amazing dogs whose lives have been transformed by our assistive devices.

Luna

Beagle

Device: Custom Prosthetic Leg

Luna lost her front leg in an accident but never lost her spirit. With her custom prosthetic, she’s back to chasing squirrels and exploring the neighborhood. Her owner says she’s more confident than ever.

Max

German Shepherd

Device: Mobility Wheelchair

After a spinal injury left Max’s rear legs paralyzed, his family feared his active days were over. His custom wheelchair changed everything. Now Max hikes trails, goes on beach runs, and participates in adaptive dog sports.

Request a Device

If your dog needs an assistive device, we’re here to help. Our application process is simple and designed to gather the information we need to create the perfect solution for your furry friend. All devices are provided completely free of charge.

What We Need

  • Veterinary assessment and recommendation
  • Dog’s measurements and photos
  • Medical history and current condition
  • Contact information for follow-up
